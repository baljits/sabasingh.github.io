Brand Development: DIGITALMUSEO is a platform for artists & art ethusiasts to share & experience digital contemporary art. Designed to resemble a museum-like experience online, the brand is developed with 3 concepts in mind: simplicity, flexibility, sophistication.

Web & App Design: the website and mobile application of DIGITALMUSEO aim to bring an art community to life through simple navigation, clean pages and a focus on the visual element.

Service Design: Researching the customer's journey through Qatar's Hamad International Airport brought to light prominent breakdown points, one being the check-in process. This was illustrated through a customer journey map & blue print.

Personal Identity: I portrayed myself through a visual element that has been inspired by the cultures that have surounded me. My personal identity is an amalgamation of digital & ethnic visual elements derived from these culture.

Digital Children's Book: This iBook explores one of Delhi's most celebrated traditional fairs, the Surajkund Mela. Blending inkpen sketches, digital designs & animations, it plays on the fair's traditional stalls & modern visitors.

Ambient Device: BLANK is an ambient device that uses sensors and gestures to provide real time information about soil nutrition and moisture levels. At a glance the user will know when is a good idea to water and add nutrition to the soil.

Event Branding: Using location design to attract local attention, TEDxEducationCity's posters, roll ups and banners used Hamad Bin Khalifa University Student Center's architecture to target students from accross Qatar.

Filming & 3D Animation:Puppenstein explores the deepest desires of a man plagued by disease & death. In an effort to balance the building violence and restraint within himself, he develops a not-so-scary army of ragged puppets.

Infographic Development: This project inlcuded analyzing one week of Whatsapp chats and illustrating essential information. This included developing character faces, categorizing content and analyzing data as a whole.

Publications and Writing

A Network Analysis on Fight Club

I worked on this research during my senior year. Using the popular movie Fight Club, I presented a network analysis research that explored etymological roots of specific compound words used in the screenplay at the renowned Sunbelt Conference in Florida, 2014.


Barbed Wire

This screenplay speaks about the disparity felt by those whose families have been separated by the border created between India and pakistan. The story explores the vastness of the problem by following the lives of two friends who fly kites together.


Auditing Carnegie Mellon's Orientation Process

I was hired by Carnegie Mellon University in Qatar to audit the orientation process through research and past data. Employing focus groups, interviews and in-depth analysis of previous years' data.
